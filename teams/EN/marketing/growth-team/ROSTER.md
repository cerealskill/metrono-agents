# ROSTER.md

## Team
Growth Team

## Objective
Fill the top of the funnel, nurture leads through every stage, and hand off sales-ready prospects to closing — running as a coordinated demand generation engine.

## Members

| Agent | Role | Responsibility |
|-------|------|----------------|
| `lead-gen` | Lead Generator | Identifies, researches, and qualifies new pipeline prospects from multiple sources |
| `sdr-outbound` | SDR | Executes personalized cold outreach sequences and books discovery meetings |
| `lifecycle-marketer` | Lifecycle Marketer | Designs and runs automated nurture journeys for leads at every funnel stage |
| `content-strategist` | Content Strategist | Produces demand-gen content (blog, ads, emails) that attracts inbound leads |

## Interaction Model

Content attracts → Lead Gen qualifies → SDR reaches out → Lifecycle nurtures until ready.

```
┌───────────────────┐      ┌─────────────────┐
│ Content Strategist│─────>│   Lead Gen      │
│(content-strategist│      │  (lead-gen)     │
└───────────────────┘      └────────┬────────┘
   (inbound)                        │ qualified list
                                    v
                          ┌─────────────────────┐
                          │    SDR Outbound     │
                          │   (sdr-outbound)    │
                          └──────────┬──────────┘
                                     │ not yet ready
                                     v
                          ┌────────────────────────┐
                          │  Lifecycle Marketer    │
                          │ (lifecycle-marketer)   │
                          └────────────────────────┘
                              (nurture → MQL)
```

## When to use
- Launching a new product or market and need to build pipeline from scratch.
- Current lead volume is too low or lead quality is inconsistent.
- You want to automate the top-of-funnel before scaling a human sales team.

## Use Cases
- **New market entry:** Content attracts interest → Lead Gen sources contacts → SDR cold sequence → Lifecycle warms remaining.
- **Outbound campaign:** Lead Gen builds ICP list → SDR sends sequences → Lifecycle follows up non-responders.
- **Inbound optimization:** Content reworks SEO → qualifies inbound signups → Lifecycle segments by intent.

## Rules
- Every lead gets a quality score before SDR contact.
- SDR only touches MQL-qualified leads — no spray-and-pray.
- All outreach is logged in CRM.
- Unsubscribes are respected immediately — no re-enrollment.
