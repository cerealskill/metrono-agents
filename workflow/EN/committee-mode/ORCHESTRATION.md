## Workflow
Committee Mode (voting + tiebreaker)

## Objective
Make decisions by voting among agents with an explicit tiebreaker mechanism.

## Roles
- **Committee members (odd number ideally).**
- **Chair/Tiebreaker.**

## Flow
1. Presentation of options.
2. Short debate with time limits.
3. Recorded vote.
4. If tie: apply tiebreaker criterion.

## Tiebreaker criteria
- Lower operational risk
- Greater reversibility
- Better impact/effort ratio
- Chair's vote (last resort)

## Deliverable
- Decision record
- Votes per option
- Final justification
