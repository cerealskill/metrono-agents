## Workflow
RACI Matrix

## Objective
Define responsibilities without ambiguity: who does, who approves, and who is informed.

## Definitions
- **R (Responsible):** executes.
- **A (Accountable):** approves and is accountable for the result.
- **C (Consulted):** provides input.
- **I (Informed):** kept informed.

## Rules
- Each task must have exactly 1 A.
- Do not start a task without R and A defined.
- RACI changes must be versioned.
