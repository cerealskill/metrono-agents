### Red Flags

1. **DSCR at 1.10x** — Below El 1.25x threshold. Most lenders will require additional reserves o Un larger down payment.
2. **100% occupancy** — Unusual y likely temporary. Model at 95% occupancy para realistic projections.
3. **Operating expense ratio at 30%** — Verify this is accurate. Multifamily typically runs 35-45%. If real expenses are 40%, NOI drops para $143,640 y cap rate falls para 5.98%.
